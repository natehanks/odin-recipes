German Pancakes

Description

German pancakes, sometimes called Dutch Babies, are freaking delicious. This recipe is going to teach you how to make the best one that I've tried yet, tweaked slightly to include even more protein.

This recipe originally came from Tastes Better From Scratch

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 Scoop Just Plain Protein Powder
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 4 Tbs Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°.
  2. Start by mixing all the ingredients in a blender.
  3. Once the ingredients are mixed, cut the butter into small chunks, and put them into a 9x13 inch pan.
  4. Put the pan with the butter into the oven for about 1 minute to melt the butter.
  5. Pull out the pan and let it cool slightly, before pooring your batter into the melted butter.
  6. Put it into the oven for 20-25 minutes, and enjoy with strawberries and powdered sugar. Or whatever you want.
